Frequently asked questions

How do I block apps?

Open LockOut → tap "Block Apps" → select the apps or categories you want to block from the picker. iOS shows you a list of apps installed on your device; LockOut never sees the actual app names.

How do I unlock a blocked app?

Tap the blocked app on your home screen. The Shield screen appears. Tap "Earn Unlock" — LockOut will open and prompt you to complete an exercise (push-ups, squats, or jumping jacks). Camera and motion detection count your reps. Once you complete the challenge, the app unlocks for your chosen break length.

Why does LockOut need camera and motion permissions?

To detect that you're actually doing the exercise. Both camera and motion processing happen entirely on your device — nothing is uploaded or shared. You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings → LockOut.

Why does LockOut need Family Controls / Screen Time authorization?

Apple's Family Controls framework is the only way third-party apps are allowed to block other apps on iOS. The first time you launch LockOut, iOS asks you to authorize this. Without it, the app can't block anything.

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one exercise challenge each day to extend your streak. Miss a day and the streak resets — unless you have a Streak Freeze available, which automatically protects you for one missed day. You earn Streak Freezes by hitting weekly goals.

What is Elixir?

Elixir is the in-app reward currency. You earn it by completing exercises, hitting streak milestones, and unlocking achievements. Higher elixir totals show on the leaderboard.

How do I add friends?

Open the Friends tab. Each user gets a 6-character friend code. Share yours with someone, or enter theirs in the "Add Friend" field. They'll get a request and once they accept, you'll appear on each other's friend leaderboards.

What do my friends see about me?

Only your display name, current streak, and total elixir. Nothing else from your account is visible. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Open LockOut → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your authentication account and all associated data (gameplay stats, schedules, exercises, leaderboard entry). The action cannot be undone.

I forgot my password

If you signed up with email + password, you can reset it via the sign-in screen. If you used Sign in with Apple or Google, recover your account through Apple or Google's normal recovery flows.

The app crashed / something isn't working

Email brianpcoleman@gmail.com with your iOS version, iPhone model, and what you were doing when the issue happened. Screenshots help.
